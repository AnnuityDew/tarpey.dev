RuleDescription
Prize Pool3rd place refund, 2nd place 2x refund, 1st place gets pot
RosterQB / RB / 2WR / TE / 2FLX / 1K / 1D / 5B
DivisionsChampions, AFC East, Referees
PlayoffsThree division winners (#1, #2, #3) and three wild cards (#4, #5, #6) make playoffs. Playoffs reseed after each round.
Consolation BracketSwiss-style tournament. Winner earns choice of draft slot next year.
SchedulingPlay divisional opponents twice. Schedule fills with non-division opponents.
Playoff Tiebreakers
  1. If two teams in the same division end up tied (i.e. 7-7), tiebreakers will happen in this order:
    1. Head-to-head record
    2. Division record
    3. Points for
    4. Points against
    5. Coin toss (a real one, not random number generated)
  2. If two teams in different divisions end up tied, tiebreakers will happen in this order:
    1. Head-to-head record
    2. Points for
    3. Points against
    4. Coin toss (a real one, not random number generated)
  3. If 3+ teams end up tied, ties are broken INSIDE each division first before comparing teams from different divisions.
