Ms. Newland's AP English Literature and Composition, Block 3A

Welcome to my digital portfolio website for the 2009-2010 school year! This is my final entry into my high school English portfolio, and it was heavily influenced by the passing of my mother and father during the course of senior year. Many pieces in here are analytical, given that this was required by the nature of the course. But there are a few pieces that I poured a plethora of emotion into, and it is my firm belief that these are the best pieces of the portfolio. I'm extremely proud of everything on this page, so please, enjoy everything you come across!

The elements of the portfolio PDF are organized in the format required by Ms. Newland. The rest of the pieces created during the year that weren't featured in the portfolio are below.
