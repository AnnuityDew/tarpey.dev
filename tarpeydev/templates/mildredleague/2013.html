2013 was the inaugural year of Mildred League. Brando was technically running the league at this point with me as co-commissioner.

Notes

  • The original name of the league was "El Futbol Drafto", and the championship game was still known as the "Drafto Bowl" at this point.
  • Bryant's 0-13 record this year stood as the worst regular season in league history until 2018, when Mildred's Redskins posted an 0-14 regular season record.
